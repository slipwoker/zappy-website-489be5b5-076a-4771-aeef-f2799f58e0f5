Why Join The Pack?

From game-day thrills to meaningful community impact, discover what makes being a Dawg Pound Collective member so special.

Free Membership, No Strings

Becoming part of our family costs absolutely nothing. We believe passion for the Browns and love for our community should be open to everyone. Just show up, wear your orange and brown, and you're one of us.

Exclusive Game-Day Specials

Enjoy members-only discounts on food, drinks, and merchandise every Sunday at Game On. From loaded nachos to ice-cold beverages, we make sure your game day experience is as delicious as it is electric.

Unmatched Fan Camaraderie

Surround yourself with the loudest, proudest, and most loyal fans in Northeast Ohio. Every first down, every touchdown, every victory tastes better when you're celebrating with your fellow Dawgs.

Community That Gives Back

We're more than football. Through our fundraisers and events, we support local schools, honor our veterans, aid women's shelters, and help every dawg find a home. Your cheers help fund real change.

What to Expect on Sundays

Every game day at Game On follows a rhythm built on camaraderie, celebration, and community. Here's how a typical Sunday unfolds when you join the Dawg Pound Collective.

11:00 AM

Arrival & Pre-Game Warm-Up

Doors open early at Game On in North Olmsted. Grab your seat, claim your favorite spot at the bar, and settle in with exclusive member-only food and drink specials. The big screens are tuned to NFL pre-game coverage, the orange and brown gear is everywhere, and the energy starts building well before kickoff. It's the perfect time to catch up with fellow fans, meet new members, and get ready to make some noise.

1:00 PM

Watch-Party Energy & Fundraising

Kickoff unleashes the full Dawg Pound roar. Every first down, every touchdown, every defensive stand is celebrated like we're right there in the stadium. Between quarters, we run our community fundraising moments — 50/50 raffles, prize giveaways, and donation drives supporting local schools, veterans, women's shelters, and dog shelters. The game day fun fuels real impact, and every member gets a chance to contribute and win.

4:30 PM

Postgame Celebration & Community

Win or lose, the Dawg Pound Collective sticks together. After the final whistle, we announce raffle winners, celebrate the day's fundraising totals, and share a toast to our Browns. It's a chance to swap stories, plan for the next watch party, and connect with the causes we support. Membership is free, the camaraderie is priceless, and every Sunday strengthens the bond between fans and our North Olmsted community.

Membership is absolutely free — just show up in your orange and brown and you're in the family.
