Why Join The Dawg Pound Collective?

This is more than just a watch party; it's a vibrant community for Browns fans who bleed orange and brown. Membership is free, the perks are exclusive, and the impact is real — supporting local schools, veterans, women's shelters, and dog rescues right here in our community.

Free Membership, No Strings

Becoming a member of The Dawg Pound Collective is absolutely free. No fees, no hidden costs — just pure Browns passion and an open invitation to join our growing family of dedicated supporters.

Electrifying Game Day Atmosphere

Imagine cheering every play, celebrating every touchdown, and sharing every thrilling moment with a crowd as passionate as you are — all within the fantastic atmosphere of Game On in North Olmsted.

Exclusive Members-Only Specials

Enjoy exclusive game day specials designed to make every Sunday even better. From food and drink deals to members-only giveaways, your membership comes with perks that elevate the experience.

Game Day at Game On

Every Sunday, The Dawg Pound Collective transforms Game On in North Olmsted into the ultimate Cleveland Browns headquarters. Feel the roar of the crowd, the pulse of every play, and the camaraderie of hundreds of fans who bleed orange and brown — all while giving back to the community we love.

  • Pregame Gathering

    Arrive early, grab your seat, and soak in the anticipation. The energy builds with every handshake and high-five as the Dawg Pound faithful unite.

  • Kickoff Intensity

    From the opening whistle to the final drive, experience every snap with a room full of passionate fans. The cheers are louder, the highs are higher, and the lows are shared together.

  • Fourth-Quarter Celebration

    When the game is on the line, the Dawg Pound rises. Win or lose, we celebrate our team and our community — because being a Browns fan is about family, fun, and making a difference.

Cheering for Cleveland, Giving Back Locally

At The Dawg Pound Collective, game day is just the beginning. We channel our Browns passion into meaningful fundraisers that support the heart of our community — because real fans show up for their city year-round.

Local Schools

We rally to fund scholarships and classroom resources for North Olmsted students, ensuring the next generation has every opportunity to succeed on and off the field.

Our Veterans

We honor those who served by directing community donations to veteran support programs, ensuring our local heroes receive the gratitude and assistance they deserve.

Women's Shelters

We stand with local women and families in need by organizing supply drives and fundraisers that provide safety, comfort, and a path forward.

Dog Shelters

Every Dawg deserves a home. We partner with local shelters to provide supplies, adoption support, and plenty of love for our four-legged friends.

Our purpose goes beyond football. It's about building a stronger, more connected community where every touchdown celebration fuels real change. When you join The Dawg Pound Collective, you're not just a fan — you're a force for good.
